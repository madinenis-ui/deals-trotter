Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Dealstrotter is a personal automation tool operated by an individual developer. It posts product deal information to a personally-owned Pinterest board and Telegram channel.

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from third parties. It only interacts with the developer's own Pinterest account via the Pinterest API to create pins on boards owned by the developer.

No user data beyond what is required for the developer's own Pinterest OAuth authentication is accessed or retained.

For questions, contact via the Dealstrotter Telegram channel: @Dealstrotter
